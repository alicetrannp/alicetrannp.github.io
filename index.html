Hello, it's Alice

Care that begins
with listening.

I'm Alice Tran, a dual board-certified Family and Psychiatric Nurse Practitioner (PMHNP-BC, FNP-BC). I provide psychiatric care to adults ages 18–60 across Virginia through telehealth, with appointments available in both English and Tiếng Việt.

I grew up between two worlds — Vietnamese at home, American everywhere else. That experience shaped how I practice. I understand what it means to carry something that doesn't have an easy name in either language, and to need a space where your whole story is welcome, not just the parts that translate neatly.

My approach is integrative and unhurried. I believe that good psychiatric care isn't just about the right medication — it's about understanding who you are, what you've been through, and what kind of life you're trying to build. Our first appointment is a full hour because your story deserves that kind of time.

I work with adults navigating anxiety, depression, trauma, ADHD, burnout, identity, and more. I have a particular passion for serving immigrant communities, first-generation Americans, and anyone who has ever felt like the mental health system wasn't quite built for them.

The approach

Three quiet promises I make
to every person I see.

01

I will listen, fully.

No rushed intakes. No one-size-fits-all checklists. Your first visit is a full hour because your story deserves the time.

02

I will care for the whole of you.

Sleep, family, faith, food, work, loneliness, purpose. Mental health lives within a life, and we will make space for all of it.

03

I will honor where you come from.

Culture, language, background, and belonging shape how we heal. Tiếng Việt is welcome here, along with all that you carry.

A note on urgency

If you are in crisis right now, please do not wait for an appointment.
Call or text 988 — the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline. Free, confidential, 24/7.
For emergencies, call 911 or go to your nearest emergency room.
