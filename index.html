About Alice

Care that begins
with listening.

I'm Alice, a dual board certified Family and Psychiatric Nurse Practitioner providing psychiatric care to adults ages 18 to 60 across Virginia.

My approach is grounded in a simple belief: healing begins when someone feels truly heard. Appointments are offered through telehealth, which means you can begin care from a place that already feels like yours.

Tiếng Việt is welcome here, and so is whatever you arrive carrying — whether named or unnamed.

The approach

Three quiet promises I make
to every person I see.

01

I will listen, fully.

No rushed intakes. No one-size-fits-all checklists. Your first visit is a full hour because your story deserves the time.

02

I will care for the whole of you.

Sleep, family, faith, food, work, loneliness, purpose. Mental health lives within a life, and we will make space for all of it.

03

I will honor where you come from.

Culture, language, background, and belonging shape how we heal. Tiếng Việt is welcome here, along with all that you carry.

Services

A small, intentional practice.

Built for depth, not volume.

Initial Visit

Comprehensive Psychiatric Evaluation

60 minutes · Telehealth

A spacious first appointment to understand your history, symptoms, relationships, and goals. We'll co-create a plan that respects your pace and values.

Follow-Up

Medication Management

30 minutes · Telehealth

Ongoing, thoughtful medication management with close attention to how you are actually feeling — not just what the chart says.

Integrated

Supportive Psychotherapy

Woven into care

Brief, focused conversations alongside medication because a prescription without context rarely leads to meaningful healing.

Culturally Attuned

Care in Tiếng Việt

For you & your family

Mental health support in Vietnamese so your parents, elders, and loved ones can be part of the conversation too.
