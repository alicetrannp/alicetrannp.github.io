About Alice

Care that begins
with listening.

I'm Alice, a dual board-certified Family and Psychiatric Nurse Practitioner (PMHNP-BC, FNP-BC) providing psychiatric care to adults ages 18 to 60 across Virginia.

I specialize in psychiatric evaluation, medication management, and psychotherapy. My approach is grounded in a simple belief: healing begins when someone feels truly heard. Appointments are offered through telehealth, which means you can begin care from a place that already feels like yours.

I grew up between two worlds, Vietnamese at home, American everywhere else. That experience shaped how I practice. I understand what it means to carry something that doesn't have an easy name in either language, and to need a space where your whole story is welcome.

Whether your concerns are new or longstanding, you deserve care that is thoughtful, personal, and centered on the whole of who you are. Tiếng Việt is welcome here, and so is whatever you arrive carrying, whether named or unnamed.

The approach

Three quiet promises I make
to every person I see.

01

I will listen, fully.

No rushed intakes. No one-size-fits-all checklists. Your first visit is a full hour because your story deserves the time.

02

I will care for the whole of you.

Sleep, family, faith, food, work, loneliness, purpose. Mental health lives within a life, and we will make space for all of it.

03

I will honor where you come from.

Culture, language, background, and belonging shape how we heal. Tiếng Việt is welcome here, along with all that you carry.

Services

Initial Visit

Comprehensive Psychiatric Evaluation

60 minutes · Telehealth

A spacious first appointment to understand your history, symptoms, relationships, and goals. We'll co-create a plan that respects your pace and values.

  • Diagnostic assessment
  • Personalized treatment plan
  • Psychoeducation & collaborative goal-setting
Follow-Up

Medication Management

Up to 30 or 50 min · Telehealth

Ongoing, thoughtful medication management with close attention to how you are actually feeling - not just what the chart says.

  • Psychiatric evaluation and diagnosis
  • Medication initiation, adjustment, and monitoring
  • Treatment planning and coordination
Integrated

Supportive Psychotherapy

Woven into every visit

Brief, focused conversations alongside medication - because a prescription without context rarely leads to meaningful healing.

  • Supportive listening and guidance
  • Psychoeducation about your condition
  • Coping strategies and skill-building
Culturally Attuned

Care in Tiếng Việt

A note on urgency

If you are in crisis right now, please do not wait for an appointment.

Call or text 988 - the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline. Free, confidential, available 24/7.
For emergencies, call 911 or go to your nearest emergency room.

Alice Tran Psychiatric Care does not provide crisis services. If you are in immediate danger, please contact emergency services.
