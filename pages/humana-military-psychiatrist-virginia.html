Insurance Information

Using Humana Military (TRICARE) for Psychiatric Care

Humana Military administers TRICARE benefits for eligible beneficiaries in the East region, which includes Virginia. TRICARE covers outpatient mental health services, including psychiatric evaluations and medication management visits with a qualified psychiatric nurse practitioner.

TRICARE telehealth mental health coverage has expanded significantly in recent years, and many beneficiaries can now receive psychiatric care via secure video visit from their home. Your cost-sharing depends on which TRICARE plan you are enrolled in -- for example, TRICARE Prime, TRICARE Select, or TRICARE For Life each have different rules for referrals and cost-sharing.

Before your first appointment, contact Humana Military at the number on your TRICARE card to confirm your mental health benefits, ask whether a referral or prior authorization is needed, and verify your cost-sharing for outpatient psychiatric telehealth visits.

What Is Covered

Psychiatric Services Covered Through Humana Military (TRICARE)

  • Initial psychiatric evaluation
  • Follow-up medication management visits
  • ADHD assessment and medication management
  • Anxiety and depression treatment
  • Insomnia medication management
  • Telehealth video visits from your home in Virginia
  • Coordination with primary care and other treating providers

Note: Coverage details depend on your specific TRICARE plan and benefit year. Always verify your benefits with Humana Military before your first appointment. Alice Tran Psychiatric Care will verify your insurance and provide a cost estimate before your visit.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Does TRICARE cover telehealth psychiatric visits?

Yes. TRICARE covers outpatient mental health services provided via telehealth, including visits with a psychiatric nurse practitioner. Telehealth mental health coverage has expanded and most TRICARE beneficiaries can now receive psychiatric care by secure video from their home in Virginia.

Do I need a referral to see Alice through TRICARE?

Referral requirements depend on your TRICARE plan. TRICARE Prime typically requires a referral from your primary care manager (PCM) for specialty mental health care. TRICARE Select generally does not require a referral for in-network providers. Contact Humana Military or check your plan documents to confirm what is required for your specific plan.

What is my cost-sharing for TRICARE psychiatric visits?

Cost-sharing varies by plan. Active duty service members enrolled in TRICARE Prime typically pay nothing for covered care. Retirees and family members may pay a copay or cost-share depending on the plan. Call Humana Military at the number on your TRICARE card for the exact cost-sharing for your situation.

I have TRICARE For Life. Am I covered?

TRICARE For Life works as a secondary payer alongside Medicare. If you have both Medicare and TRICARE For Life, Medicare pays first and TRICARE For Life may cover some or all of your remaining cost-sharing. Contact Humana Military to confirm your outpatient mental health benefits under TRICARE For Life.

What psychiatric conditions does Alice treat for TRICARE patients?

Alice evaluates and treats anxiety, depression, ADHD, insomnia, and other adult psychiatric conditions. She prescribes and manages psychiatric medications and provides referrals to therapy when appropriate. She welcomes veterans, active duty family members, and all TRICARE-eligible beneficiaries.

How do I verify that Alice is in my TRICARE network?

You can search the Humana Military provider directory at humanamilitary.com or call the member services number on your TRICARE card. You are also welcome to contact our office at (703) 791-9099 to verify your specific plan before scheduling.
