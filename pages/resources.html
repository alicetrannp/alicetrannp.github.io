Resources

A small, curated library
for the in-between days.

A few trusted resources for when you need support between visits or are not yet ready to reach out.

Crisis Support

If you need help right now

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline

Free, confidential crisis support available 24/7. Call or text 988 from anywhere in the US. You do not have to be suicidal to call — if you're struggling, they are there.

988lifeline.org →

Crisis Text Line

Text HOME to 741741 to connect with a trained crisis counselor. Free and confidential, available 24/7 for any type of crisis.

crisistextline.org →
Mental Health Education

Learn & understand

NAMI (National Alliance on Mental Illness)

Comprehensive education, support groups, and advocacy resources for individuals and families navigating mental health conditions.

nami.org →

Anxiety & Depression Association

Evidence-based information about anxiety, depression, OCD, PTSD, and related conditions. Includes a therapist finder tool.

adaa.org →

CHADD (ADHD Resources)

Education and support for individuals with ADHD and their families. Includes webinars, articles, and local support groups.

chadd.org →

Postpartum Support International

Resources and support for perinatal mood and anxiety disorders, including postpartum depression, anxiety, and OCD.

postpartum.net →
