About this practice

Small city, very real pressure.

Falls Church is one of the smallest independent cities in the United States, but it sits at the intersection of some of the most demanding suburban pressure in the country. The schools are among the most competitive in Virginia. The cost of living reflects the proximity to DC. And many Falls Church residents are commuting into Arlington, Tysons, or the city itself, carrying work stress home to a community that expects a lot. Falls Church is also home to Eden Center, the heart of the region's Vietnamese community, and Alice, who practices in both English and Tiếng Việt, sees many patients and families from that community who prefer care in their own language.

Alice Tran, PMHNP-BC provides telehealth psychiatric evaluations, medication management, and supportive therapy for adults in Falls Church and the surrounding area. She is board-certified in psychiatric mental health, sees patients in English and Vietnamese, and conducts all appointments via secure video, no traffic, no parking, no waiting room.

Psychiatric Evaluation

A 60-minute first visit to understand your history, symptoms, and goals before building a care plan together.

Medication Management

Ongoing follow-up visits (30–50 min) to review how treatment is working and adjust as needed.

Supportive Therapy

Brief, focused psychotherapy woven into every appointment, not a separate service, part of the visit.

English & Vietnamese

Full care in Vietnamese for the Falls Church Vietnamese community. No interpreter needed.

Common questions

Falls Church patients often ask

What insurance do you accept?

See Rates for most updated in network insurances and current fees.

Do you see patients who have never been to a psychiatrist before?

Yes, frequently. The first visit is a conversation, not a test. You do not need a prior diagnosis or a referral. You just need to show up.

Can you treat ADHD in adults?

Yes. Alice provides comprehensive adult ADHD evaluation and prescribes medication when appropriate, including stimulant medication via telehealth under current Virginia regulations.

How quickly can I be seen?

Check the online booking calendar for real-time availability. If you do not see a slot that works, call or text (703) 791-9099.

Local to Falls Church

Care within reach of Eden Center and Seven Corners.

Falls Church holds two worlds in a few square miles: the Little City itself, with its walkable downtown and top-ranked schools, and the corridors around Seven Corners and Eden Center, the cultural heart of the Vietnamese community on the East Coast. Alice offers psychiatric care in both of the languages this community lives in, English and Tiếng Việt, with no interpreter needed, and family members welcome in the conversation when that helps.

The Fairfax office at 3060 Williams Drive is about fifteen minutes down Route 50 or Route 29 from Falls Church, with free on-site parking. Telehealth visits by secure video are available anywhere in Virginia, which many Falls Church patients prefer for follow-ups: a 30-minute visit over a lunch break instead of a drive on Route 7.

Common reasons Falls Church residents reach out include anxiety that never fully switches off, depression carried quietly through a busy family life, adult ADHD recognized after a child’s diagnosis, and the particular stress of living between two cultures and two sets of expectations. Most major insurance is accepted, including Medicare, Medicaid, and Anthem HealthKeepers, and new patients are typically seen within one to two weeks.
