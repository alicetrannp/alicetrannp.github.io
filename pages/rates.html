In-Network Coverage

Insurance We Accept

Alice Tran Psychiatric Care is currently in-network with the following insurance plans. If your plan is listed, your visits may be covered, contact your insurer to confirm your mental health benefits before booking.

✓ Aetna ✓ Medicaid ✓ Medicare ✓ Carelon ✓ Beacon Health Options ✓ Anthem BCBS Virginia ✓ Anthem HealthKeepers ✓ Humana Military ✓ Cigna

Additional plans coming soon. We are actively credentialing with more insurance providers. If your plan is not listed, self-pay rates apply and a superbill can be provided for out-of-network reimbursement. Contact us to check on the latest status.

Insurance Verification: Patients are responsible for verifying coverage for requested services and will be responsible for any claims that are not paid within 30 days.

Out-of-Network Benefits

If you have out-of-network benefits, you may be eligible for partial reimbursement from your insurance company. A detailed superbill (itemized receipt) will be provided after each session, which you can submit to your insurer directly. Please note that Alice Tran Psychiatric Care does not bill out-of-network claims on your behalf.

Tips for checking your out-of-network benefits:
Call the member services number on the back of your insurance card and ask: "Do I have out-of-network mental health benefits? What is my out-of-network deductible and reimbursement rate for telehealth psychiatric services?"

What to Expect in Your Follow-Up Appointment

Policies

Cancellation Policy

If you do not show up for your scheduled appointment, or if you cancel with less than 24 hours' notice, you will be required to pay a $100 cancellation fee.

Life happens - if you need to reschedule, please do so as early as possible so the appointment time can be offered to someone on the waitlist. Thank you for your understanding.

Your Rights

Good Faith Estimate
No Surprises Act

Under the No Surprises Act, you have the right to receive a Good Faith Estimate explaining how much your care will cost before you schedule your appointment. This estimate will include expected charges for services reasonably anticipated to be provided.

If you receive a bill that is at least $400 more than your Good Faith Estimate, you can dispute the bill. For questions or to request a Good Faith Estimate, please contact us.

A note on urgency

If you are in crisis right now, please do not wait for an appointment.

Call or text 988 - the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline. Free, confidential, available 24/7.
For emergencies, call 911 or go to your nearest emergency room.

Alice Tran Psychiatric Care does not operate 24/7 and does not provide crisis services. Emails, voicemails, text or portal messages are typically responded to within 24 to 72 business hours.
