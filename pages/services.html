What I offer

Every visit is
built around you.

01
Comprehensive Psychiatric Evaluation

Initial Visit  ·  60 minutes  ·  Telehealth

A spacious first appointment to understand your history, symptoms, relationships, and goals. We'll map out what's been happening and co-create a plan that respects your pace and values.

  • Diagnostic assessment
  • Personalized treatment plan
  • Psychoeducation & collaborative goal-setting
02
Psychiatric Medication Management

Follow-Up  ·  Up to 30 or 50 min  ·  Telehealth

Ongoing, thoughtful medication management with close attention to how you are actually feeling - not just what the chart says.

  • Medication adjustments
  • Symptom monitoring
  • Care plan reassessment
03
Supportive Psychotherapy

Integrated  ·  Woven into care

Brief, focused conversations that sit alongside medication management - because a prescription without context rarely leads to meaningful healing.

  • Psychoeducation
  • Behavioral activation
  • Emotional processing
  • Coping skills support
04
Care in Tiếng Việt

Culturally Attuned  ·  For you & your family

Mental health support in Vietnamese so your parents, elders, and loved ones can be part of the conversation too. Culture and language matter in healing.

  • Full sessions in Vietnamese
  • Family-inclusive approach
  • Culturally informed care
05
Lab Testing

Diagnostic Support  ·  When clinically appropriate

Lab testing can offer valuable insight into factors that may be affecting your mental health, including underlying medical conditions, nutrient deficiencies, hormonal imbalances, and other physical health concerns. This information can help guide accurate diagnosis, support personalized treatment planning, and promote the safest and most effective use of medications.

06
Genetic Testing

Pharmacogenomics  ·  Personalized medicine

Genetic testing can provide helpful insight into how your body may process certain psychiatric medications based on your unique genetic makeup. This information may help guide medication selection, reduce trial and error, and support a more personalized treatment approach. While genetic testing does not diagnose mental health conditions, it can be a useful tool in creating a safer and more effective treatment plan.

07
Telehealth Consultations via Video

HIPAA-Compliant  ·  Secure & Private

We offer secure psychiatric care through virtual appointments, so you can receive support from the comfort and privacy of your home. All visits are conducted through a HIPAA-compliant video platform designed to protect your confidentiality. Telehealth allows for greater flexibility and supports consistency in care.

  • Secure, encrypted video visits
  • No commute, no waiting room
  • Available across all of Virginia

A note on urgency

If you are in crisis right now, please do not wait for an appointment.

Call or text 988 — the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline. Free, confidential, available 24/7.
For emergencies, call 911 or go to your nearest emergency room.

Alice Tran Psychiatric Care does not operate 24/7 and does not provide crisis services. Emails, voicemails, text or portal messages are typically responded to within 24 to 72 business hours.
