Insurance Information

Using Your HealthKeepers Benefits for Psychiatric Care

Anthem HealthKeepers is Anthem Blue Cross Blue Shield's HMO plan offered to individuals and employer groups in Virginia. HealthKeepers plans cover mental health and psychiatric services under the same federal parity rules as medical benefits, meaning your psychiatric care is covered at the same level as a visit to your primary care doctor.

Alice Tran Psychiatric Care is in-network with Anthem HealthKeepers. This means you pay your in-network copay or coinsurance rather than out-of-network rates. Your exact cost depends on your specific plan's deductible status and cost-sharing structure.

Before your first appointment, call the member services number on the back of your HealthKeepers card to confirm your mental health benefits, verify telehealth coverage, and ask about your specialist copay for outpatient psychiatric visits.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Does HealthKeepers cover telehealth psychiatric visits?

Yes. Most HealthKeepers plans cover telehealth mental health visits, including psychiatric appointments. Confirm telehealth mental health benefits by calling the member services number on your card before your first appointment.

Do I need a referral from my primary care doctor?

HealthKeepers HMO plans may require a referral from your primary care physician before seeing a specialist, including a psychiatric provider. Check your Summary of Benefits or call Anthem to confirm whether a referral is required for your specific plan before scheduling.

What is my copay for a psychiatric visit with HealthKeepers?

Copay amounts vary by plan. Call the member services number on your HealthKeepers card and ask for your in-network mental health specialist copay to get the exact amount for your plan.

What is the difference between HealthKeepers HMO and OAPOS?

HealthKeepers HMO requires you to use in-network providers and typically requires a primary care referral for specialists. HealthKeepers OAPOS (Open Access POS) gives you more flexibility to see out-of-network providers at a higher cost. Both plan types are accepted at Alice Tran Psychiatric Care.

How do I find out if Alice is in my HealthKeepers network?

You can search for in-network providers at anthem.com using your HealthKeepers plan, or call the member services number on the back of your card. You can also contact our office directly and we will verify your benefits before your first visit.
