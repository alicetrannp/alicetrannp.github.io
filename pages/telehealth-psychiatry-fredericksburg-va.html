About this practice

Telehealth psychiatry for Fredericksburg.

Fredericksburg and its surrounding counties are home to some of America's longest commutes: I-95 north at dawn, home after dark. That life breeds burnout, anxiety, and strained families, while local psychiatric availability stays thin. Telehealth turns a two-hour round trip into a video call from your kitchen.

Alice Tran, PMHNP-BC grew up between two worlds, Vietnamese at home and American everywhere else. She provides psychiatric evaluations, medication management, and supportive therapy fully in Vietnamese or English, with no interpreter needed. When it helps, parents and family members can join the conversation, so care feels clearer and more comfortable for everyone involved. Khám và tư vấn hoàn toàn bằng tiếng Việt.

The practice is based in Fairfax, and nearly all Fredericksburg patients are seen by secure telehealth, with the same full evaluations, prescriptions, and follow-up care as an office visit. Most major insurances are accepted, including Medicare and Medicaid, and new patients are typically seen within one to two weeks.

Quick answers

What Fredericksburg patients want to know

Do you really conduct visits in Vietnamese?

Yes, completely. Alice is fluent in Vietnamese and conducts full evaluations, medication discussions, and follow-ups in Tiếng Việt with no interpreter. Family members are welcome to join when that feels helpful.

Can you see my parents, too?

Alice sees adults ages 18 to 65. For parents and elders within that range, she provides culturally attuned care in Vietnamese, including gentle first conversations for family members who have never talked about mental health before.

What insurance do you accept?

Most major plans, including Aetna, Anthem, Cigna, Medicare, and Medicaid. See Rates for the current list and self-pay pricing.

Is the office far from Fredericksburg?

The office is in Fairfax, so nearly all Fredericksburg patients choose secure telehealth, which is available anywhere in Virginia and works exactly like an in-person visit: same evaluation, same prescriptions, same follow-up care.
