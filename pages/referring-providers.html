For Referring Providers

A Reliable Psychiatric Referral
for Your Adult Patients

Alice Tran, PMHNP-BC accepts referrals from primary care providers, therapists, OB/GYNs, and other clinicians across Virginia. New patients are typically seen within 1–2 weeks.

How to Refer →
Scope of practice

Who to refer

The practice serves adults ages 18 to 65 for outpatient psychiatric evaluation, medication management, and supportive therapy, in person in Fairfax or by telehealth anywhere in Virginia. Care is available fully in English or Vietnamese with no interpreter needed. Commonly referred concerns include:

Anxiety disorders Major depression Adult ADHD evaluation PTSD & trauma Bipolar disorder OCD Postpartum & perinatal mood Insomnia Medication consultation

A good fit

Stable outpatients needing evaluation, a diagnosis clarified, medication initiated or optimized, or a prescriber to work alongside their therapist. First appointments are a full 60 minutes.

Not a fit

Patients under 18 or over 65, active crisis or imminent safety concerns (please direct to 988 or the nearest ED), court-ordered evaluations, and long-term benzodiazepine maintenance, which this practice does not prescribe.

Referral process

How to refer a patient

1. Fax a referral

Fax demographics, insurance, and any relevant notes to (804) 807-9399. The office will contact your patient directly to schedule.

2. Or have them self-book

No referral paperwork is required. Patients can book online or call/text (703) 791-9099. Online booking takes about five minutes.

3. Coordination of care

With patient consent, you will receive an initial evaluation summary and relevant updates. Alice coordinates readily with PCPs and therapists, and is glad to co-manage.

4. Questions first?

Email info@alicetrannp.com or call (703) 791-9099 to discuss whether a patient is a good fit before referring.

Practice details

What your patient can expect

Availability & wait time

New patients are typically seen within 1–2 weeks. Office hours are Monday through Thursday, 9am to 4pm. Visits are in person at 3060 Williams Drive, Suite 300, Fairfax, VA 22031 or by secure telehealth anywhere in Virginia.

Credentials & supervision

Alice Tran is dual board-certified (PMHNP-BC, FNP-BC) through the ANCC, and trained under the clinical supervision of Dr. Errol Segall, MD, a psychiatrist with more than 50 years of experience and former clinical faculty at Georgetown University School of Medicine. See About for full credentials.

Insurance

In-network with Aetna, Anthem BCBS Virginia, Anthem HealthKeepers, Cigna, Medicare, Medicaid, Carelon, Humana Military, and Beacon Health Options. Transparent self-pay rates are published on the Rates page.

Prescribing policies

Evidence-based prescribing including stimulants for ADHD where clinically appropriate under current Virginia telehealth regulations. Short-term benzodiazepines only when clinically indicated; no long-term benzodiazepine maintenance. Lab and pharmacogenomic testing available when useful.
