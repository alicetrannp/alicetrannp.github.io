Accessibility Statement

Last updated: May 2026

Alice Tran Psychiatric Care is committed to ensuring that this website is accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. Accessibility matters deeply to us care should be reachable by all.

Our Commitment

We strive to make alicetrannp.com usable by as many people as possible, including those who use assistive technologies such as screen readers, keyboard navigation, or voice control. We aim to meet the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, as our accessibility benchmark.

What We've Done

To support accessibility, we have taken the following steps:

  • Used semantic HTML elements to support screen reader navigation
  • Provided descriptive alt text for meaningful images
  • Ensured sufficient color contrast between text and backgrounds
  • Designed the site to be fully usable on mobile devices of all sizes
  • Made all navigation accessible by keyboard
  • Used clear, plain-language content throughout
  • Offered content in both English and Vietnamese

Telehealth Accessibility

Our telehealth appointments are conducted through Doxy.me, which supports standard web browsers and does not require a download or account. If you have specific accessibility needs related to your appointment such as captioning, language assistance, or alternative communication please let us know when scheduling and we will do our best to accommodate you.

Known Limitations

We are a small, independently run practice and continue to improve this site over time. If you encounter any accessibility barriers, we want to know. Your feedback helps us do better.

Request an Accommodation

If you need information from this website in an alternative format, or if you have a disability-related accommodation request for your care, please contact us:

  • Phone / Text: (703) 791-9099
  • Email: info@alicetrannp.com

We will do our best to respond within 2 business days and work with you to find a solution.

Feedback

We welcome feedback on the accessibility of this website. If you experience any difficulty accessing content, navigating a page, or using a feature, please tell us. We take all reports seriously and are committed to continual improvement.
