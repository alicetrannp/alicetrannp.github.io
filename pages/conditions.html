Who I help

I work with adults ages 18–60 across Virginia through telehealth. Whether your concerns are longstanding or new, I offer a full first hour to understand your history, symptoms, and what matters most to you before we decide together on next steps.

🌀

Anxiety

Persistent worry, racing thoughts, physical tension, and avoidance - anxiety is one of the most common and most treatable conditions in psychiatry.

Learn more → 🌧️

Depression

More than sadness - depression can look like numbness, exhaustion, loss of interest, or simply feeling like a dimmer version of yourself.

Learn more → 🕊️

Trauma & PTSD

What happened to you doesn't have to define the rest of your life. Trauma-informed care that works at your pace and respects your nervous system.

Learn more → 🔥

Burnout

Running on empty is not a personality trait. Burnout is what happens when you give too much for too long without enough support or recovery.

Learn more → ⚡

ADHD

It's not laziness or lack of trying. ADHD is a neurological difference that affects attention, focus, and executive function - and often goes unrecognized in adults.

Learn more → 💨

Panic Disorder

Panic attacks are terrifying - and they are treatable. The fear of the next one doesn't have to keep shrinking your world.

Learn more → 🍂

Grief & Loss

Grief doesn't follow a schedule. When it becomes too heavy to carry alone - whether from death, divorce, or any significant loss - support is here.

Learn more → 🌱

Life Transitions

Big changes - even good ones - can shake you loose. Career shifts, moves, relationship changes, and becoming a parent all deserve real support.

Learn more → 🪞

Identity & Self-Worth

You don't have to earn the right to exist. Questions of who you are, where you belong, and what you're worth can be explored in a safe, affirming space.

Learn more → 🤱

Postpartum

You're allowed to struggle even while you love your baby. Postpartum depression and anxiety are medical conditions - not signs of failure.

Learn more → 😴

Insomnia & Sleep

Everything is harder when you're not sleeping. Sleep is the foundation of mental health - and chronic insomnia deserves real attention.

Learn more → 🔁

OCD

The thoughts are not who you are. OCD is one of the most misunderstood and most treatable mental health conditions - the right care can quiet the noise.

Learn more → 🌸

Perinatal Mental Health

Pregnancy is not always the happiest time - and that's okay. Mental health care during pregnancy matters just as much as prenatal vitamins.

Learn more → 🌓

Bipolar Disorder

Mood cycling is real - and it can be stabilized. Accurate diagnosis and the right treatment plan make a meaningful difference in daily life.

Learn more → 💢

Anger & Emotional Dysregulation

Anger is information. When it feels uncontrollable, it usually means something else is going on beneath the surface - something worth understanding.

Learn more → 💛

Self-Esteem & Confidence

The voice that says "you're not enough" can be quieted. Low self-esteem has roots - and those roots can be understood and worked with.

Learn more → 🌏

Cultural & Intergenerational Stress

You can love your family and still be shaped by things that hurt you. Cultural and intergenerational patterns deserve a space that truly understands them.

Learn more → ✈️

International & Immigrant

Building a life here while carrying everything from there. Care available in English and Tiếng Việt, with deep understanding of the immigrant experience.

Learn more →
