Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Alice Tran Nurse Practitioner LLC ("we," "our," or "us") collects and uses information when you visit alicetrannp.com. It applies to this website only. Your protected health information (PHI) as a patient is governed separately by our HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices, which you will receive at intake.

Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

When you contact us or request an appointment, you may provide:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • General reason for seeking care
  • Any other information you choose to share

Information collected automatically

When you visit this website, basic technical information may be collected automatically, including your browser type, pages visited, and general geographic location (city/region level). This is standard for any website and helps us understand how the site is used.

Cookies

This website uses only essential functional cookies necessary for the site to operate. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings without affecting your ability to use this site.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and schedule appointments
  • Communicate with you about your care
  • Improve this website

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Platforms

We use the following HIPAA-compliant platforms to deliver care:

  • IntakeQ for online appointment scheduling and intake forms (IntakeQ Privacy Policy)
  • Doxy.me for telehealth video visits (Doxy.me Privacy Policy)

These platforms have their own privacy policies and Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) in place to protect your information in accordance with HIPAA.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information submitted through this website. All data transmitted through this site uses SSL/TLS encryption (the padlock you see in your browser). However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Please do not send sensitive health information (such as symptoms, diagnoses, or medications) through general email or contact forms. Use our secure patient portal through IntakeQ for sensitive communications.

HIPAA & Your Health Information

As a psychiatric healthcare provider, we are required by law to maintain the privacy of your protected health information (PHI). Your rights as a patient and our obligations are described in our HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices, provided to you at the time of your first appointment.

Children's Privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted information through this site, please contact us so we can remove it.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
